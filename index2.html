Why Choose Say It?

Instant Text-to-Speech

Just take a photo, and Say It reads the text for you instantly with high accuracy.

Simple & Accessible

Designed specifically for the visually impaired with an intuitive, easy-to-use interface.

Works on Any Printed Text

Read medication labels, menus, street signs, books, and more. Works completely offline!
