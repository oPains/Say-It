Our Journey

From a personal project to a global accessibility solution.

2023

Project Inception

Omar begins developing Say It as a personal project to help his grandmother read after her vision loss. The first prototype focuses on offline OCR and text-to-speech.

2024

Beta Testing

After months of development and testing with local accessibility organizations, Say It launches beta testing with 500 users in egyptian retirement homes.

Late 2024

Official Launch

Say It officially launches on Android with support for 10 languages and immediately gains traction in the accessibility community. 1,500 users join in the first month.

2025

Global Impact

Say It reaches 40,000+ users across 18 countries. Partnerships with accessibility organizations in progress, and the app becomes a trusted tool in the visually impaired community.

Future

Expanding Horizons

We're working on iOS support, additional languages, and partnerships to serve even more users worldwide. The journey continues!
